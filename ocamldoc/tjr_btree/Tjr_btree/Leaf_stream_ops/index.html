Module Tjr_btree.Leaf_stream_ops

A leaf stream is a linear sequence of the leaves in a B-tree, used for iterating over all the bindings in the tree. Leaf stream operations: make a leaf stream; get the list of (key,value) pairs associated to "current" leaf; step to the next leaf.
